Project Overview

The Real Estate Sales Management System is a comprehensive desktop application designed to streamline property sales operations, manage customer relationships, track payments, and generate detailed reports. This powerful system serves as a centralized hub for real estate agents and property developers.

Built with enterprise-grade desktop technologies, the system provides intuitive interfaces for managing property listings, handling booking fees, processing payments, and maintaining customer communication through automated notifications. The platform ensures efficient workflow management while maintaining data security and integrity.

Core Features

Comprehensive functionality to manage all aspects of real estate sales operations

Property Management

Add, edit, and manage property listings with detailed information, pricing, and availability status.

Booking Fee Management

Process and track booking fees with automated calculations and printable receipts for customers.

Payment Processing

Comprehensive payment tracking with multiple payment methods, due dates, and automated reminders.

Customer Management

Track customer information, communication history, and sales status throughout the buying journey.

Sales Memo System

Generate and manage sales memos with detailed transaction information and official documentation.

Notification System

Automated notifications for payment reminders, status updates, and important sales milestones.

Sales Analytics

Comprehensive reporting on sales performance, customer status, and revenue tracking with visual charts.

Document Printing

Print booking fees, receipts, sales memos, and reports with professional formatting and branding.

Transaction History

Complete audit trail of all transactions, payments, and customer interactions for compliance.
